## Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Title**: GigShield AI
- **Subtitle**: Parametric Micro-Insurance for the Gig Economy
- **Tagline**: Fully Automated. Zero-Touch. Hyper-Personalized.
- **Visuals**: GigShield Logo, a Zomato/Swiggy worker in the rain.

## Slide 2: The Problem (Delivery Persona)
- **Target Persona**: "Ravi, 26, Zomato Delivery Partner in Mumbai."
- **The Pain**: Ravi earns ₹500/day. When heavy monsoon hits or Section 144 restricts movement, his income drops to zero instantly. 
- **The Gap**: Traditional insurance expects him to pay a massive ₹15,000 yearly premium and wait 30 days for a claim. He can’t afford either. He operates on weekly cash flow.

## Slide 3: The GigShield Solution
- **Micro-Policies**: Weekly bite-sized coverage (e.g. ₹15/week for ₹800 coverage).
- **Parametric Triggers**: No paperwork. We track Open-Meteo weather APIs and local anomaly data. If predefined thresholds are met (e.g. >10mm rain), the policy triggers algorithmically.
- **Zero-Touch Payouts**: The claims are processed and sent directly to the worker's UPI instantly.

## Slide 4: AI & Neural Network Architecture (Core Tech)
- **Dynamic Pricing**: Instead of static grids, we use a lightweight ML Engine (`brain.js`).
- **Inputs**: (1) Zone Danger (e.g. Flood Prone), (2) Live Weather/Climate forecasts, (3) Past Claims Density.
- **Output**: The neural network perfectly calculates risk exposure and quotes a hyper-localized weekly base premium (₹5 - ₹50), making it incredibly fair for safe workers while isolating liabilities.

## Slide 5: The 7-Layer Fraud Detection Engine
- **How We Protect The Platform**:
  1. **GPS Haversine Restriction**: Ensuring the worker's coordinates are precisely within the disruption radius.
  2. **Biomechanical Telemetry**: Analyzing device accelerometer variance to detect spoofed static farming devices.
  3. **GPS Node Spoofing Guard**: Calculating velocity between history pings to detect physically impossible movement.
  4. **Historical Weather Cross-Check**: Validating the worker's claim against the 24-hr satellite archive from Open-Meteo to prevent third-party API spoofing.

## Slide 6: Business Viability (The Math)
- **Is this profitable?**: Yes. The AI ensures the **Loss Ratio** is always contained. 
- **Micro-Subscription Model**: 10 million gig workers paying ₹20/week creates massive recurring revenue pools.
- **Eliminated Overhead**: Because claims are 100% parametric and handled via Zero-Touch UPI, we have **literally ZERO manual claim adjusters**, saving millions in operational costs compared to legacy firms.
